Astrophysics Art

In the summer of 2023, I had the pleasure of working with Dr. Zach Berta-Thompson as a Colorado Scholar of Astrophysics and Art (CSAA). My project focused on the crucial (and often overlooked) contributions of women and gender minorities in this field, educating viewers on how diversity and inclusion lead to paradigm-shifting science. I want to emphasize how much representation matters for both old and new generations in astrophysics through my art. I drew each portrait/collage by hand in Procreate, and it was fun to experiment with different brushes and textures to emulate the cosmos. This art is free to download/print for personal use :-).

Annie Jump Cannon

Pioneer astronomer who developed the stellar classification system still in use today.

Mae Jemison

Physician, engineer and NASA astronaut who became the first Black woman to travel in space.

Jessica Mink

Astronomer and software developer known for her work on astronomical data analysis.

Aomawa Shields

Astronomer specializing in exoplanet habitability and climate modeling.

Claudia Alexander

Planetary scientist and the final project manager of NASA's Galileo mission to Jupiter.

Henrietta Leavitt

Astronomer who discovered the relationship between the luminosity and period of Cepheid variables.
